Hissa 3: Time Nahi, Energy Manage Kijiye

Series: Jeevan Udaan: 5-Hisssiyon Ki Series (Motivation & Life-Improvement) • Prakashit: 2025-08-29 • Hissa 3/5

Jeevan Udaan Series

Productivity ka asli raaz: apni energy waves pakadna. Jab dimaag sabse tez hota hai, us waqt sabse mehnat waala kaam. Jab energy low, tab admin/chores.

3 Energy Levers: (1) Neend 7–8 ghante, (2) Hydration + light stretches, (3) Single-tasking – notification off, 25–50 min deep focus.

Apne peak slots ko name dijiye – “Rocket Hours”. In hours me sirf high-value tasks allow honge. Baaki sab ko din ke dusre hisso me shift kijiye.

Power Move: Afternoon slump se pehle 10-min walk + paani. Dimaag ko oxygen milti hai, focus wapas aata hai.
