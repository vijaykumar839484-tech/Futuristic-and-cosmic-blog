Hissa 5: Anti-Fragile Life – Jitna Pressure, Utni Taaqat

Series: Jeevan Udaan: 5-Hisssiyon Ki Series (Motivation & Life-Improvement) • Prakashit: 2025-08-29 • Hissa 5/5

Jeevan Udaan Series

Anti-fragile ka matlab: sirf survive nahi, balki stress se aur majboot hona. Iske liye optionality zaroori hai – ek se zyada income streams, ek se zyada skills, ek se zyada network nodes.

Stress Inoculation: Chhote controlled challenges set kijiye – cold shower, 24h social detox, no-spend weekend, public speaking practice. Body aur mind dono ko training milti hai.

Money Buffer: 3–6 mahine ka emergency fund + basic insurance. Financial stability se creative risk lena asaan hota hai.

Power Move: Har setback ka 1-page debrief likhiye – kya hua, kyun hua, agali baar kya alag karenge.
