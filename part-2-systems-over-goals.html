Hissa 2: Goals Se Zyada Powerful – Systems

Series: Jeevan Udaan: 5-Hisssiyon Ki Series (Motivation & Life-Improvement) • Prakashit: 2025-08-29 • Hissa 2/5

Jeevan Udaan Series

Goals finish line dikhate hain; systems running track banate hain. Jab hum sirf goal pe tik jaate hain, to beech ka safar boring lagta hai. System banaiye: trigger → action → reward. Bas aaj ka chakkar perfect kijiye.

Example: “30 din me 5 blog publish.” Iska system: har subah 30-min reading, 20-min ideas capture, sham ko 25-min draft. Ek chhoti checklist bana kar fridge ya desk pe chipka dijiye.

Anti-friction tips: (1) Tools ko ready rakhiye (notebook, template), (2) time-block kal raat hi set, (3) start line ko asaan banaiye – “sirf 5 minutes”.

Power Move: Weekly review me sirf teen cheezein check karen – kya chala, kya nahi chala, agle hafta ek kya badlenge.
