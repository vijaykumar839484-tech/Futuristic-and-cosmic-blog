Hissa 4: Skill Compounding – Chhote Steps, Bada Asar

Series: Jeevan Udaan: 5-Hisssiyon Ki Series (Motivation & Life-Improvement) • Prakashit: 2025-08-29 • Hissa 4/5

Jeevan Udaan Series

Compounding sirf paise me nahi, skills me bhi hota hai. Ek core skill (writing/coding/sales) ko 2–3 micro-skills (research, storytelling, analytics) ke saath jodiye. Yeh combo aapko rare aur valuable banata hai.

Portfolio Sprint: 30 din me 3 mini projects. Har project ko 1-page case study me convert karke LinkedIn/portfolio par dikhaiye.

Power Move: “Learning Loop” set kijiye – learn → do → teach. Jo seekha use 48 ghante ke andar kisi ko samjhaiye; knowledge cement ho jaata hai.
